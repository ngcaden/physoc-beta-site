Solution Creation Guide

The PhySoc Wiki uses Overleaf to edit and render solution to past papers.

Step 1. Sign Up or Sign In at Overleaf.

Step 2. Click on New Project to create a new document.

Step 3. Choose the Blank Paper template.

Step 4. Type in your solution in LaTeX

Step 5. Click on Share.

Step 6. Copy the Read & Edit Link.

Step 7. Add the link to Imperial Wiki's Solution Link.
